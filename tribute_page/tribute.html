Biography

Steven Paul “Steve” Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and co-founder of Apple Inc. He was a pioneer of the personal computer revolution and transformed industries such as computers, music, phones, and digital publishing.

His vision, innovation, and attention to detail made Apple one of the most valuable companies in the world. Steve Jobs believed in simplicity, creativity, and perfection.
